2.1 Transformer-Based LLMs
Transformer-based LLMs [2, 17, 70] are composed of stacked
layers of multi-head attention, feed-forward networks (FFNs),
and normalization layers. During inference, computation
proceeds autoregressively in two phases: prefill and decode.
The prefill phase parallelizes computation over the input
prompt, resulting in high arithmetic intensity due to large
matrix multiplications operated over multiple tokens. On
